Bread

Bread is the most basic type of flour foods. It can be filled or eated bare.

Ingredients

  • Flour
  • Water
  • Yeast
  • Oil

Steps

  1. Mix warm water with yeast.
  2. Add flour and oil into the mix and knead mix into a dough.
  3. Cover the cooking bowl with a towel and let it rise for 30-60 minutes.
  4. Form risen dough into small balls and cook for 25 minutes in 200 C.
