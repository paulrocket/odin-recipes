Pizza

Pizza is a delicate dish from italy. It consist of breadlike base, tomato sauce and for cheese filling.

Often pizza can be filled with various food objects like meat and vegetables or even mushrooms! Only your imagination is the limit when making a pizza.

Ingredients

  • Flour
  • Water
  • Yeast
  • Olive Oil
  • Tomato Sauce
  • Salt
  • Cheese
  • Optional Fillings

Steps

  1. Mix yeast with warm water.
  2. Add flour and salt into the mix and knead it into a dough.
  3. Finish with olive oil.
  4. Mold the dough into a round, thin shape and bake in max temperature for 1 minute(until you see bubbles forming in the surface).
  5. Add tomato sauce, optional fillings and cheese on top of the dough.
  6. Bake in 150 C until the cheese is golden brown. Let pizza sit 5 minutes before serving.
